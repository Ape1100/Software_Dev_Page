Innovating Travel and Tech Solutions

Quantum Novitas is a tech startup focused on revolutionizing travel experiences with innovative tools. We specialize in creating apps and platforms that cater to modern travelers, providing safety, convenience, and enhanced experiences.

Our flagship project, the Smart Travel App, integrates lodging comparisons, local attraction suggestions, budgeting tools, and safety insights for a seamless travel experience.

Our Projects

Smart Travel App

The global travel industry is undergoing a significant transformation, with modern travelers demanding more personalized, data-driven solutions to enhance their experiences. In regions like South America, where tourism is on the rise, there is a growing need for apps that help travelers find budget-friendly lodgings, manage travel expenses, and anticipate the local cost of goods. Safety concerns are also at the forefront of travelers' minds, making real-time data on crime levels and safe practices a valuable asset. Additionally, recent traveler reviews provide invaluable insights into accommodations and destinations, allowing users to make informed choices. Our Smart Travel App addresses these needs by offering an all-in-one platform for finding lodgings, budgeting, comparing prices, tracking safety, and accessing up-to-date reviews—all essential features for the modern traveler navigating unfamiliar regions.

SAAS CRM for Small Businesses

Small businesses are increasingly recognizing the importance of customer relationship management (CRM) tools to streamline their operations, boost customer retention, and drive growth. However, many of the CRM solutions on the market are designed for large enterprises, offering complex features that smaller companies neither need nor can afford. This creates a significant opportunity for a customizable, lightweight CRM that caters specifically to small businesses, allowing them to manage customer interactions, sales, and marketing efforts efficiently without the overwhelming complexity of full-scale applications. Additionally, a CRM that can scale with a growing business ensures long-term usability, reducing the need for costly migrations to larger platforms as the business expands. Our solution provides flexibility, allowing small businesses to implement just the features they need now, with the ability to add more as their needs grow—empowering them to focus on building strong customer relationships without the burden of oversized systems.

Why Invest with Us?

The global travel app market, valued at approximately $1.29 billion USD in 2023, is projected to grow at a 10.6% compound annual growth rate (CAGR) through 2030. At the same time, the SaaS (Software as a Service) market for small businesses, especially in the CRM space, is experiencing rapid expansion. Valued at over $140 billion USD globally in 2022, the SaaS market is expected to continue growing, driven by the increasing demand for flexible, scalable software solutions tailored to small businesses. The current market conditions present a unique opportunity to capitalize on rising demand for both personalized travel assistance and customizable CRM tools that are adaptable to growing business needs. More travelers are seeking safe, budget-conscious experiences, while small businesses increasingly require subscription-based CRM systems that offer scalability without overwhelming complexity. To seize this market niche, we are actively entertaining potential partners who can help bring these applications to market in a timely fashion. With the right investment support, we can accelerate development, capture market share, and establish a dominant presence before the market becomes saturated—creating long-term value for our partners and delivering cutting-edge solutions to our users.
